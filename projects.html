Project 2

SIPEKA

SIPEKA (Sistem Pakar Kesehatan) is a thesis project that implements an expert system to diagnose potential diseases in active smokers using the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) and Certainty Factor (CF) methods. AHP is applied to determine the priority weighting of symptoms, while CF is used to calculate the level of confidence in diagnostic results. This system aims to support early detection and increase awareness of smoking-related health risks.
